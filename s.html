Rules & Guidelines

1. Event Duration

The Vibe Coding contest is a 48-hour event. The timer starts for you individually as soon as you click the "Deploy Countdown" button. This action is final and cannot be reversed.

2. Submission

All project files must be submitted via the "Submission Portal" *before* your personal 48-hour timer expires. Late submissions will be automatically rejected by the system.

3. Theme

The theme of the contest is **"Transform"**. Your project must interpret this theme. Be creative!

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us via the submission form, including team member names, project details, and contact links (GitHub). All data is transmitted securely to a Google Sheet for judging purposes only.

How We Use Information

Your information is used *only* for the purpose of organizing the Vibe Coding event, judging submissions, and contacting winners. We will not sell, distribute, or lease your personal information to third parties.

Terms of Service

By participating in the Vibe Coding event, you agree to these terms.

1. Eligibility

This event is open to all students of Vibe Coding College. Teams can consist of 1 to 4 members.

2. Submissions

All submissions must be original work created during the 48-hour event window. Pre-existing projects are not allowed. The use of open-source libraries and frameworks is permitted. Use of AI tools must be disclosed.

3. Code of Conduct

All participants must adhere to a spirit of respectful collaboration. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.

4. Judging

All judging decisions are final. Projects will be judged based on creativity, technical implementation, adherence to the theme, and overall presentation.

Support Matrix

If you encounter a critical system error or require assistance, please use the following channels:

  • Technical Support (Discord): Join the `#vibe-coding-support` channel for real-time help from mentors.
  • Submission Form Issues: Contact an admin directly on Discord (e.g., @AdminName).
  • General Questions: Use the `#vibe-coding-general` channel.

Please do not wait until the last minute to report a submission issue. We are here to help!
